The Best Movies According to Snehal

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Neerja

This is daring real life story of a air hostess-Neerja who saved many lives from a terror attack on a passenger plane which was hijacked.

Mission Majnu

It is a story of an Indian spy who saved India by revealing the production of nuclear bomb by Pakistan.

Mission Raniganj

An amazing story of saving 71 miners from the oldest coal field of India at Raniganj.
